The Curator:

I am Hashim Nyasuro an Art Curator, Culture manager, and creative organizer. I curate spaces that are human designed for a specific creative event, exhibition or festival. I have started a platform to educate young creatives in the music  and art space called "Mzizi ni Msingi" whereby young artists express themselves with the art and get to be mentored on upskilling,  music business and career growth. The stage helps creatives network in a community whereby they share the experience and educate themselves with other social aspects. 

I also do research on Tanzania arts and music in order to tell stories through exhibition and art shows which depicts our history and heritage through our own experiences and currently working in telling stories of our culture and transforming knowledge by bridging generations through our art and music platforms.

I enjoy nature, and looking forward to curate more experience around the ntaural habitat.
